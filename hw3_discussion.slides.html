Euclidean Distance¶


$$ distance = \sqrt{\Sigma_{b=1:totBins}(PSTH - trial)^2}$$ Dimensions:
PSTH: 1 X (tot_bins $*$ Neurons)
Trial: 1 X (tot_bins $*$ Neurons)

Information Confusion¶


$$I = H_a + H_b - H_{ab}$$ $$H_a = \text{row entropy} | H_b = \text{col entropy} | H_{ab} = \text{joint info}$$

Entropy¶

  • Find probability of given row/column (p(a), p(b))
  • Find probability of each entry in confusion matrix (p(ab)) $$H_a = -\Sigma{p(a)log_2(p(a))}$$
